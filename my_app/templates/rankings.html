Methodology

The k factor of my ELO equation is 32 however it can go up to 36 if the fighter got a knockout or a submission, also depending on how early the win was. A first round KO shows the difference in levels between two fighters: they are very far apart.

However I only take UFC fights into account.

ELO rankings in sports like UFC are not always accurate due to the low volume of matches, however they show interesting insights.
